Who I Am

A designer who thinks beyond pixels

Hi, I'm Alex Rivera — a Product Designer with 5+ years of experience crafting thoughtful digital products. I've worked across startups and enterprise teams, leading end-to-end design from discovery to delivery.

My approach is rooted in user empathy and data-informed decisions. I believe great design solves real problems, delights users, and drives measurable business outcomes.

Before design, I studied psychology — which gives me a deep appreciation for how people think, behave, and make decisions. I bring that perspective to every project I touch.

How I Work

My design process

01
🔍
Discover & Research
I start with deep user research, stakeholder interviews, and competitive analysis to understand the problem space fully before touching any design tools.
02
💡
Define & Strategize
Synthesizing research into clear insights, I define user personas, journey maps, and design principles that guide every decision downstream.
03
✏️
Design & Prototype
From lo-fi wireframes to pixel-perfect high-fidelity screens — I iterate rapidly, build interactive prototypes, and validate ideas early and often.
04
🚀
Test & Deliver
Usability testing, dev handoff with complete design specs, and post-launch monitoring ensure the final product truly serves its users.
